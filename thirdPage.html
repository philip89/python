5a month agoI love this gym. It's really big, has a big variety of equipment and machines. Even though it can be really busy during working days 5-7 pm. No swimming pool, but they can offer a sauna, good showers and a big selection of different classes free of charge if you are a member. People say that there is a hassle while registering, but it just seems so. As you have to register online on their web site before you come to the gym many questions can arise. But, I would offer to come to the gym, if you are not sure how to do registration. Downstairs, ate the entrance there are 2 registration machines. Don't forget to bring your phone, debit/credit card detail as you will need them to make a registration. Usually, staff members can see people through the cameras and if they notice you can't do it yourself, they will help you. If not, ask somebody to call staff member and they will definitely give you a hand. Staff are really helpful there and eager to go extra mile.
